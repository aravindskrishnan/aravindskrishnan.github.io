TEAM STRUCTURE

LIKE OUR PROJECTS, THE TEAM ALSO HAS A SOPHISTICATED STRUCTURE. THE TEAM CONSISTS OF 2nd AND 3rd YEAR UNDERGRADUATE STUDENTS FROM NITC CALICUT. THE TEAM IS THEN FURTHER SUB DIVIDED INTO SUBTEAMS WITH DESIGN LEAD FOR EACH SUBTEAMS. 
JUST LIKE OUR CARS, EACH SUBTEAM WORKS LIKE AN INDEPENDENT PART WITH PERFECT COORDINATION WITH THE ENTIRE TEAM.

EXCITED ? HOP ON THEN, JOIN TEAMUNWIRED.

Give yourself an exciting opportuinity to be part of something that makes time and people stand still. 

1

WHO CAN JOIN ?

1st Year students of undergraduate course of all programmes of offered by NIT Calicut can join the team.

2

HOW CAN I JOIN THE TEAM ?

Inductions for the team will start in the 2nd semester. The same will be informed through our social media handle and orientation presentations at 1st year hostels. The students have to register themselves through the link and give a priliminary examination, after which there will be a interview. The selected 12-18 students will get to work on the projects.

3

IS THE INDUCTION BRANCH SPECIFIC ?

NO. The inductions are not branch specific. We require students from all branches of engineering. What we require is passion, students will be selected on the basis of their knowledge and passion alone.
